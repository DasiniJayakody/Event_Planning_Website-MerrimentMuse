Garden Wedding Venues

Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo

Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo is a perfect wedding destination of any size that wants to embrace luxury and natural beauty. The hotel boasts up to 12 stunning venues, indoor and outdoor, with the lakeside garden is the most desirable. Upon arriving, you'll suddenly feel the tranquil ambience, and you'll realize that you're far from the hustle and bustle of the capital city. The feeling of a tropical breeze, natures at every corner, and the calming view of the lake will calm your heart and mind as you say sacred vow to your loved one.

Website: https://www.cinnamonhotels.com/cinnamonlakesidecolombo
Address: 115, Sir Chittampalam A. Gardiner Mawatha, Colombo 02, Sri Lanka
Capacity: Up to 600 guests
Contact: +94 11 2 161 161

Beach Wedding Venues

Colombo Fort Hotel

If you're planning to have a wedding ceremony at a pure beach not far away from the capital, then Colombo Fort Hotel is one of the best wedding venues in Sri Lanka. The hotel has direct access to one of Sri Lanka's most beautiful stretch of beaches with a shaded lawn lying next to the beach. It offers you a chance to say “I do” at such a beautiful place and hold a wedding feast at one of their regal ballrooms that will impress your guests. Added with their impeccable service on assisting wedding to every little detail, you'll enjoy a stress-free memorable experience.

Website: https://www.colomboforthotels.com/
Address: 8-2/1,York Arcade Building, Leyden Bastian Road, Colombo 01, Sri Lanka
Capacity: Up to 200 guests
Contact: +94 11 2326 927

Hotel Wedding Venues

Shangri-la Colombo

For a dream of an extravagant wedding that becomes the talk of the town, Shangri-La Hotel Colombo is the most desirable. Here, you'll have the chance to celebrate a wedding at unique and luxuriously styled venues with a dedicated team who will help make your special day a memorable one. The main pillar-less Shangri-La Ballroom can host up to staggering 1400 guests with their crowning classic design. A more intimate wedding can be held either at Lotus Ballroom, Outdoor Lawn, or Spice Function Rooms.

Website: http://www.shangri-la.com/colombo/shangrila/
Address: 1 Galle Face, Colombo 2,, Sri Lanka
Capacity: Up to 1400 guests
Contact: (94) 11 788 8288

Church Wedding Venues

Kyoto Northern Church

All Saint Church Borella is known for its magnificent Gothic architecture and has been the favourite wedding place for a traditional and religious ceremony. It features colorful interior, stained glass windows above the altar depicting Mother of Perpetual Help. Any wedding celebrated here will be cherished forever just like its history that began from 1884.

Restaurant Wedding Venues

Kaema Restaurant

Led by the celebrity Chef Darshan Munisada, Kaema is one of the best restaurant wedding venues in Sri Lanka that embraces outstanding gastronomic experience. While the cuisine is native-inspired taken from the island's aromatic spices and unique ingredients merge with old cooking techniques as well as colonial influences, the design of the dining hall is uniquely authentic and modern at the same time. Take in the views over the Indian Ocean while you say “I do” to your loved one.

Hilltop Wedding Venues

Sankalana Flora

Looking for a breathtaking and unforgettable wedding experience, Sankalana Flora is the right choice to make. Beautifully nestled on a hilltop of Kandy mountainous region, Sankalana Flora embraces the beauty and fragrance of nature any love celebration held at their ground. You can also have a picture-perfect Poruwa design with the background of mountains that will marvel anyone who witnesses your vow. Hundreds of wedding dreams were created at this fantastic place no wonder it's considered as one of the best wedding venues in Sri Lanka.

Colonial Wedding Venues

Tamarind Hill Gate

Take in the charm of a UNESCO World Heritage Site for an unforgettable wedding experience. The 250-year-old manor house offers enchanting celebration at their venues from the breezy central courtyard with the old house as the background to the opulent hall with its old-world charm. Further, you'll also have the freedom of the whole house, including two of their main suites, perfect for you and your family to stay before and after the wedding.

Capacity: Up to 250 guests
